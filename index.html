Common Questions

Expert FAQ's

Find quick answers to common queries about our engineering and design services.

What engineering services do you provide?

We provide comprehensive MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) design, HVAC systems, Fire Fighting, and complete Structural Engineering solutions for residential and commercial projects.

Do you provide services for overseas Pakistanis?

Yes, we specialize in coordinating with overseas clients. We provide 3D designs, regular updates, and online consultation to ensure your project at home is handled professionally.

What is the typical project timeline?

Project timelines depend on the scope. Initial design drafts are usually delivered within 7-10 business days, while detailed MEP layouts may take longer depending on complexity.

Do you offer energy-efficient and sustainable designs?

Absolutely. We prioritize sustainable engineering practices, including energy-efficient HVAC systems and solar-ready electrical designs to reduce long-term operational costs for your building.
